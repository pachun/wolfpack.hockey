This policy applies to all information collected through the Too Many Men App.

Information we collect

Too Many Men accounts are created with a phone number. Phone numbers are only used for signing in and password resets. We don't send promotional text messages. We never call you.

If you enable notifications, we must store a token to send them. We never use notifications for marketing.

Crash reports are used to identify and prevent software bugs which cause the Too Many Men App to crash. We also measure how often features are used in order to gauge interest.

Security

We store information in a password protected database. We encrypt data in-transit using the industry standard TLS protocol.

Information usage

It's simple: We do not share or sell any of your information. All information kept is for the sole purpose of making Too Many Men App useful for its users.

Too Many Men App and its API are open source.
